Spinach Artichoke Dip

A delicious, cheesy and fragrant spinach dip

Spinach artichoke dip is a delicious way to get any party started. This crowd-pleasing spinach is guarnateed to be a hit with your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Artichoke hearts
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Soften your cheese before you mix the ingredients. Otherwise, it won't incorporate well

  2. Mix everything together before you add the spinach and artichokes. You will want the cream cheese mixture well-blended first so the veggies stay intact.

  3. Squeeze out as much moisture as you can before you add the spinach so the dip doesn't get runny.

  4. Store it in the fridge for a day or two, then bake it right before serving
