Cranberry Orange Cookies

An orange-flavoured cranberry cookie that are tart and delcious.

These cranberry orange cookies are the perfect holiday cookies to have with your friends and family. Coated with a sweet glaze, it will leave your loved ones wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Orange
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Canberries
  • Walnuts

Steps

  1. Cream the butter and sugar together. Beat in the egg, juice, zest.

  2. Whisk the dry ingredients in another bowl, then add the dry mixture to the wet mixture.

  3. Stir in the cranberries and nuts.

  4. Drop the cookies on a baking sheet and bake until the edges are golden.

  5. Mix the confectioner's sugar, juice, and zest to make the glaze.

  6. Spread the glaze over the cooled cookies.
